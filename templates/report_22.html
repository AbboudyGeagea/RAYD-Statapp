Modality ➜ AE Source ➜ Top 5 Procedures
A hierarchical breakdown of study volume across the selected period. Starting from total studies, it branches by imaging modality (CT, MR, US…), then by the DICOM AE title that received the study, and finally shows the top 5 procedure descriptions per AE. Node sizes reflect study count.
Top Referring Physicians (Study Volume)
The 10 physicians who referred the highest total number of studies in the selected period. A single physician may refer the same patient multiple times — this chart counts studies, not patients.
Physician Loyalty (Unique Patient Count)
The 10 physicians with the broadest patient reach — ranked by the number of distinct patients they referred. A high unique count relative to study volume indicates the physician refers many different patients rather than repeat visits.
Case Status Distribution
Count of studies grouped by their current DICOM study status (e.g. Read, Unread, Dictated). Helps identify backlogs or workflow bottlenecks.
Gender (Rose)
Proportion of studies by patient gender (M / F / Unknown) in the selected period. Rose chart segments are sized by study count.
Age Group (Wave)
Distribution of studies across patient age groups. Each point on the wave represents the total study count for that age band, helping identify the most active patient demographics.
